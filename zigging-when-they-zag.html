The Anti-Strategy Strategy

Rule #1

When everyone goes digital, go analog. When everyone automates, add human touch.

Rule #2

Fast when they're slow. Slow when they're fast. Never match their tempo.

Rule #3

Complicate the simple. Simplify the complex. Always do the unexpected.

Rule #4

When they whisper, shout. When they shout, whisper. Volume is relative.

Rule #5

Add friction where they remove it. Remove friction where they add it.

Rule #6

The best practice is to have no best practice. Optimize for surprise.

What They Do:

  • Follow trends
  • Optimize everything
  • Reduce friction
  • Scale uniformly
  • Measure success
→

What You Do:

  • Set trends
  • Surprise everything
  • Add character
  • Scale uniquely
  • Define success

Success Stories of Doing it Wrong

Dollar Shave Club

Sold razors with comedy when everyone else sold with masculinity.

Result: $1B acquisition

Cards Against Humanity

Made a game about being horrible when games were becoming wholesome.

Result: Cultural phenomenon

Tesla

Sold cars online when everyone said you need dealerships.

Result: Industry revolution

Liquid Death

Sold water like beer when health brands whispered wellness.

Result: $700M valuation
