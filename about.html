About This Guide

Welcome to the “About” page for the PDO Research Framework. Here you’ll find the background on why we created this interactive guide, the vision behind its design, and how it can help purpose‐driven organizations enhance their brand research process.

Our Mission

The PDO Research Framework is built to empower mission-driven organizations with a structured, easy‐to‐navigate, and visually engaging guide. Our goal is to translate complex research methodologies into clear, actionable steps that any team can follow.

Why We Built This

Brand research for purpose-driven organizations can feel overwhelming—there are countless tools, phases, and measurement frameworks. We designed this site to:

  • Simplify the 5‐phase process: Provide an at‐a‐glance overview, with interactive “phase cards” that let you dive deeper into each stage.
  • Compare tools side by side: Use dynamic charts to visualize benefits vs. challenges, impact frameworks, and research methods.
  • Engage through interactivity: Offer toggles, dropdowns, and responsive charts so you can explore what matters most to your organization.

How to Use This Guide

Each section of the homepage corresponds to a phase or toolkit in your research journey:

  1. Framework: See the 5‐phase research process and click any phase to scroll directly to its toolkit or case examples.
  2. Foundations: Learn the core principles of purpose-driven brands: characteristics, leadership roles, and how to weigh benefits vs. challenges using the interactive chart.
  3. Toolkit: Dive into research methods (both quantitative and qualitative) for gathering stakeholder insights.
  4. Strategy & Creative Solutions: Explore brand‐architecture models and creative best practices for purpose-driven storytelling.
  5. Measurement & Iteration: Compare B Impact Assessment, IRIS+, and UpMetrics frameworks via the radar chart, and read about each framework’s focus.
  6. Case Studies: Scroll down to see real-world examples of brands like Patagonia, TOMS, and Warby Parker, and how they’ve integrated purpose into their strategy.

Who We Are

This guide was created by a coalition of brand strategists, designers, and purpose-driven consultants. We believe the most powerful brands are built on genuine impact, and we aim to make rigorous research accessible to every organization striving for meaningful change.
