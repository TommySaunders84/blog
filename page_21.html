Body & Paragraph (IBM Plex Sans)

This is large paragraph text (text-lg). Ideal for introductions and featured excerpts. It provides a comfortable reading experience for key content. Font is 'IBM Plex Sans'.

This is standard body text (text-base). It's used for the main content of articles and detailed descriptions. The line height is optimized for readability in long-form content.

This is smaller text (text-sm). Often used for meta information like author names, dates, categories, and captions. It provides secondary information without distracting from the main content.
