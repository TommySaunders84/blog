The Science of Beautiful Mess

Neuroscientists have found that moderate disorder actually boosts creativity. A University of Minnesota study showed that people in messy rooms came up with more creative solutions than those in tidy spaces.

Why? Because chaos forces your brain to make new connections. To see patterns where others see pandemonium. To find gold in the garbage.
